Thanet Gaming Club

Welcome to Thanet Gaming Club - The club built for gamers by gamers!

Whether you're a Mario-kart master, a Fortnite fanatic, a retro collector or if you just love a game of FIFA, then we are the group for you!

We meet once a week on a Wednesday at "The Lodge" community centre based in Westgate-on-sea

Everyone is welcome, regardless of age or ability. And the best bit...?

It's totally free to join!

What do we play?

Each week we provide a full gaming take-over of the rooms at the Lodge in Westgate-on-sea! With weekly tournaments, prizes to be won, retro consoles to play, guest speakers and refreshments available to purchase too!
